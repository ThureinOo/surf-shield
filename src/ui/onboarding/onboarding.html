🛡 Surf Shield is protecting you

No setup needed — protection is already on for every site.

What it blocks

  • Ads, trackers, and popunder ad networks
  • Popups that open without a real click, and hijacked new tabs
  • Invisible overlays that steal your clicks
  • Rapid redirect chains on shady download sites
  • Phishing lookalike sites (fake PayPal, Google, banking pages…)
  • Risky file downloads (.exe and friends are paused so you can confirm)

The toolbar button

Click the shield icon on any page. The red number on the icon shows how many attacks were stopped on that tab.

🔒
Trust this site — turns Surf Shield off for a site that broke, until you remove it from Trusted sites.
🚫
Block — never load this site again in your browser. Undo any time from the popup’s Blocked sites list.
🔍
Check on VirusTotal — not sure about a site? One click shows what 90+ security scanners say about it.

If a page warns you

A red “Dangerous page blocked” screen means Surf Shield stopped a phishing site or forced redirect. Go back to safety is almost always the right choice. “Proceed anyway” trusts the site for 1 hour only.

Settings live behind the gear (⚙) in the popup — you can switch any protection off individually. Surf Shield collects no data; everything stays in your browser.
